Feel · per pattern

Each pattern can carry its own feel — keep A tight, let C fall apart. LINK edits all four together. Ghosts and dropouts are sampled fresh every pass, so the loop never plays the same way twice.

Auto fill

On the fill bar the machine leans in: extra ghosts and ratchets build through the back half, velocities push up, dropouts ease off, and it may crash an open hat into the downbeat. FILL in the transport queues one manually.

Chaos routing

Choose which sounds each randomness type is allowed to touch. Ratchets on hats and snare stay musical; ratchets on the kick get gabber fast.
